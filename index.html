South Africa

South Africa, officially known as the Republic of South Africa (RSA), is the southernmost country on the African continent. It is renowned for its diverse landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and significant history. The country has three capital cities: Pretoria (executive), Bloemfontein (judicial), and Cape Town (legislative). With a population of over 62 million people, South Africa is known for its cultural diversity, with 11 official languages including Zulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, and English. The country is famous for its natural beauty, including landmarks like Table Mountain and Kruger National Park, and has a complex history marked by the apartheid era, which ended in 1994.
